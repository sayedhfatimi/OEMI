Welcome to the Online Encyclopedia for Mathematical Identities

This Wiki is designed as an easy access encyclopedia for well known and more importantly for unknown mathematical identities.

Submissions and changes can be posted via the Github repository: https://github.com/sayedhfatimi/OEMI approved submissions will automatically sync into the wiki.

This is still very much a work in progress and I will hope to integrate better LaTeX searchability as well as improved tagging and categorisation for finding solutions and identities much easier and faster.
